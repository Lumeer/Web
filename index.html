Data Treasure

Lumeer can extract, record, and process your data. He allows you to manage your data easily and provides the right answers to your questions. He is sort of a Google on your own data. Through various active views and editors of the data, you can always find the best way to manipulate the information. It is also possible to put data into any realtions, track audit log and all changes made in the past.

Crunching

Lumeer understands your data. He can display them to you in most suitable way. He even allows you to edit your query results and data matrices. Lumeer thinks about the value of your data while you are asleep and searches them for hidden values to provide you a fresh morning executive summary. Lumeer gives you suggestions and advices on how to best organize the data so they can be used efficiently.

View details »

Business Intelligence

Lumeer tries to point out the most unusual aspects of your data in charts and reports through which you can (in most cases) directly manipulate the data. You can even start entering your data in a form of a report. Lumeer can accept emails and extract data from their content like you would do. Lumeer can also drive business process based on your data. Data mining and machine learning are just his top notch tools.
