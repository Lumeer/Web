There is a better way

Lumeer is like a colleague who can help you expedite your tasks so that you can have more personal time to spend doing what you enjoy (e.g. family, friends, sports, hobbies, etc.).

Data Input

You can enter your data as easily as into a table. Spreadsheet data limits are not a problem. You can define columns and footers with functions, ask your colleague to complete a piece of the table, or set who can read and write your data. You can later see a complete history of changes.

Search and View

You can search for your data as easily as in any search engine like Google. You can see your data in a way that makes the most sense to you - in a calendar, map, Gantt chart, agile board, graph of relations, as multiple connected tables, etc. Simply choose what is right for you.

Not only you can view your data in different perspectives, you can interactively edit them in their visual form.

When any of your data evolves over time, you can add a time dimension and see the changes or work with past values.

Relations

Rows of tables usually fit into a context of other data. However, it is not suitable to put all the related information in a single table. To solve that, you can add relations between rows in different tables. You can also specify rules for an automated relations creation. The relations can then be used in various perspectives and for data analysis.
