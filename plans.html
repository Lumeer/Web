Pricing

All Inclusive

All our plans include all the features available for you to immediately know what Lumeer can do for you. Features, once developed, does not introduce any additional operation costs. This plan just limits the number of vCPU, memory, drive space, and network resources.

Still, we want to support students, individuals, non-profits and startups by this plan. So that you can start with Lumeer for real cheap. In this plan, we do not guarantee any support. Although, you can always send us an email because we are curious hearing how to improve our service.

Business

A small or medium sized business who want to use Lumeer with full confidence, reasonable recoures, and basic level of support will want to go with this plan.

When Lumeer starts delivering real value to you and your business, we understand that you need some more guarantees about performance, data integrity, security etc. For the price of the program, we are able to cover more resources and provide email support to you.
