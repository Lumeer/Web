Get Started with Lumeer

Try it here…

Or follow 3 simple steps

Step #1: A simple start

Pick a template

Select the template that best matches your use case. This is just a starting point, you can customize it to fit your exact workflow and needs.

Step #2: Time to customize

Make it your own

Decide what’s important for your team. Use columns to capture and visualize all the info you need. Track tasks and time, see who’s in charge of what, mark locations, add text, numbers, durations, dates, colors, tags, and much more.

Step #3: Get to work

Share the joy

Invite your team, assign work items to them, and let them fall in love with the irresistibly visual and fully transparent tool. Keep all your projects, tasks, clients, orders, invoices, offers, files, briefs, checklists, etc. in a single place and save useless meetings and painful email threads.

All the building blocks

Still curious? Here is a quick overview of what Lumeer does…

See All Features >

Tables

Start with a good old table. Create columns and set their types to fit your data.

Search & filter

Lumeer is like Google for your data. Use advanced filters or fulltext search and quickly find what you are looking for.

Gantt

Visualize any data on a timeline. Group them to categories and make changes easily.

Hierarchy & dependencies

Indent rows in a table to create a hierarchy. Visualize dependent records in a timeline.

Calendars

Save meetings, tasks and events to a calendar. Use multiple calendars or combine everything into a single one.

Dates

Store dates in multiple formats and work with them easily in the reports.

Durations

Log event durations or task estimates in a human-readable format.

Boards

Turn your tasks into cards on a board and move them between the columns.

Assignees

Select one or multiple users responsible for the task.

Maps

View anything with an address or coordinates on a map.

Addresses & coordinates

Enter addresses from all around the world, or set the location with exact coordinates.

Charts

Visualize the data in a line, bar or pie chart and drill down into aggregated values.

Pivot tables

Gain new insights or produce detailed reports with the most advanced pivot table.

Saved views

Configure a table, chart, board or other perspective, save it as a view and invite colleagues to collaborate.

Bookmarks

Save frequently used views as bookmarks and open them with one click.

Restrict access to sensitive information

Create saved views with user-based filter queries, so that everybody sees only what they need (and nothing else).

Linked tables

Instead of copying, link related records together and prevent the mess caused by duplicate data.

Functions

Create your own functions in a visual function builder. Forget SUM’s and COUNT’s – no keywords needed!

Automation

Set up rules that are executed when records are created, updated or deleted – even the linked ones!

Templates

Save time with ready-to-use templates. Choose from a wide variety of templates for many common use cases.

Categories & labels

Select a unique value from a dropdown as a category, or allow multiple ones and use them as labels.

Data import

Create brand new tables or import existing ones from CSV files.

Icons & colours

Organizations, projects, tables – everything has its own customizable icon and colour, enabling you to navigate quickly.

Sequences

Index your records with automatically generated sequences.

Rich text documents

Every table cell can be turned to a document. Format the data with a rich text editor.

File attachments

Upload file attachments to every record – they’re just another column in the table.

Projects

Group and link relevant tables and views together in one project.

Organisations

Access all company’s, department’s or team’s projects in one place.

Integrations

Integrate your data and processes with other tools using Zapier.

Data privacy

Privacy of your data is important to us. Lumeer is fully compliant with GDPR.

99.9% uptime

Stable and secure, Lumeer runs on servers in certified data centers.

Regular backups

Mistakes happen, but we won’t let them ruin your data.
